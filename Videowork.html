My Video Work

This video was a project done for SMAD 202, foundations of Audio Visual Storytelling. It is based around my home in Harrisonburg and provides a montage of different rooms and shots tied together with transitions and editing. It was the first project done in the class and was done individually.

This video was also done for SMAD 202. It was the final project for the class and it is a short narrative about a man that gets lost in the woods. The ending features a little twist if you watch it all the way through. We shot the entire film in the JMU arboretum. The project was done as a group rather than individually.
