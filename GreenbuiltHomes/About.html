Our Mission

GreenBuilt Homes is a family-owned home building business backed by over 40 years of residential building and construction knowledge. Our small company takes exteme pride in the workmanship that goes into our homes. This is why we always make sure to pay close attention to detail executed by everyone involved in the construction process, thereby ensuring a home that is superior in quality and value to other homes.

Where do we operate?

GreenBuilt Homes builds new homes in Arlington County, the City of Falls Church, and close-in Fairfax County. We can also custom build on your lot in Northern Virginia.

Building Homes, Not Houses

At GreenBuilt, we build homes, not houses. We realize the choice of neighborhood is an increasingly important factor in the home buying process. Your lifestyle is as important as the ideal features that you are looking for in a prospective home making it essential to not only have the details of a potential new home, but also the neighborhood in which it is located and where you will eventually live. Considerations such as local schools, amenities, transportation, traffic, weather, and the surrounding community are all key aspects of selecting the ideal home and location for you and your lifestyle. If you have any questions regarding the Arlington area, or if you have questions relating to specific neighborhoods, GreenBuilt Homes is here to help.

Why should you build green?

With climate change growing in scope, building green is more important than ever. But building green doesn't just benefit the enviorment, it helps you too!

Studies have shown green buildings;

  • Consume less energy
  • Are material efficient
  • Use cross ventilation reducing costs
  • Have healthier occupants
  • Cost less
